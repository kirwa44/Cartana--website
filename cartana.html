About Cartana

Cartana is a student-built website inspired by Jumia, designed to simulate a simple online business platform where users can explore and inquire about various products. The website was created as part of a practical academic project to demonstrate basic website creation skills using only HTML. Although it does not have a backend or live transactions, Cartana showcases how e-commerce websites work in terms of structure, categories, and layout.

The aim is to provide a learning tool for beginner developers and a sample design for those interested in building online business platforms. Future improvements may include adding real-time cart functionality, database integration, and payment gateway simulation.
